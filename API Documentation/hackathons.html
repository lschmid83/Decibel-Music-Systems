Accessing Support


If you have any specific queries or encounter problems along the way, you can contact us via IRC or speak to a developer in person if we have a representative at the hack, or via Skype.

For IRC support:
   IRC (Internet Relay Chat) : install an IRC client (Colloquy, mIRC, AndroIRC)
   Server : chat.freenode.net
   Channel : #decibel
   To join, type : /join #decibel

To contact us via Skype, search us by username DecibelMusicSystemsLtd or by email on api@decibel.net. We'll be online at your service!

If you wish to contact us in advance with some general problems, you can alternatively just email us at api@decibel.net which will direct you to everyone on the team.

We may have some live support on-site at the hackathon. Lookout for the Decibel logo on the back of someone's shirt!
