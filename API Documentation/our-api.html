Decibel API

Our API can tell you that:

  • The bootleg Thin Lizzy album, Remembering Part 1, is recorded from both a radio broadcast from Augora Theatre, Cleveland in May 1976 and Live King Biscuit Radio recordings from Tower Theatre, Philadelphia in October 1977.
  • Phil Lynott's Rickenbacker 4001 was manufactured between 1961 and 1981 as a deluxe model of the 4000.
  • Thin Lizzy is on Twitter, Facebook, Songkick, Soundcloud and Myspace. They have links to biographies and press contacts available.
  • Live and Dangerous was no. 50 in Kerrang!'s 100 Greatest Heavy Metal Albums of All Time. A review of the album can be found on the BBC website (links provided). The album can bought here (links provided).
